How it works

Pick a date on the calendar and donate that dollar amount to support the JWMHS Orchestra. Example: choose the 5th → $5. The blank boxes are “any amount”—give what you can. Every gift helps with music, instruments, and our spring trip.

About the musician

Meet Ella

Ella is a dedicated violist in the JWMHS Orchestra. She’s earned recognition across our county and within the program for her leadership and musicianship.

  • All-County Orchestra violist for four consecutive years (6th–9th grade) with plans to audition again this year.
  • First‑chair violist as a sophomore, leading a section that includes upperclassmen.
  • Featured soloist in multiple ensemble and orchestra performances.
