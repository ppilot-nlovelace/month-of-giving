About Ella

Meet Ella

Ella is a dedicated violist in the JWMHS Orchestra. She’s earned recognition across our county and within the program for her leadership and musicianship.

  • All-County Orchestra violist for four consecutive years (6th–9th grade) with plans to audition again this year.
  • First-chair violist as a sophomore, leading a section that includes upperclassmen.
  • Featured soloist in multiple ensemble and orchestra performances.
About the Fundraiser

How It Works

To support our orchestra, Ella is launching her Month of Giving fundraiser! It’s simple: pick a date on the calendar and donate that dollar amount (the 10th = $10). The first three boxes and the bottom-right box are any amount.

  1. Tap a date.
  2. Enter your name (optional) and tap Open Venmo to donate. The day is automatically marked as taken.

Every dollar helps our orchestra access the resources to grow our musical skills — thank you for your support!
